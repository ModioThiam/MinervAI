MinervAI, named after the roman Goddess of wisdom Minerva, is an intelligent companion designed to enhance the reading experience. By leveraging advanced AI models (Gemini API), this tool helps students engage with their reading assignments more effectively. It offers personalized assistance in summarizing, quizzing, and explaining complex concepts, turning passive reading into an interactive learning experience.
